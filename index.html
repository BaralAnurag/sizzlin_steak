Create Your Own Sizzlin' Meal

Sizzlin' Steak is a homegrown brand under Teriyaki Boy Group Inc. (TBGI). It was launched in 2007 to provide the experience of Japanese steak dining in the country. It offers high quality beef, special sauces, and a hot-plate system served within an environment that puts a premium on product quality and service speed.
The Company, under the new management, is repositioning the brand not only to serve Japanese Steak dining but a variety of sizzling meat dishes. It plans to expand its store network of both food court and casual dining formats to serve young professionals in the area. It aims to serve these professionals sizzlin' meals at everyday affordable prices.
